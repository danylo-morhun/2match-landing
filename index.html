about 2match

2match is a tinder-like app that makes choosing what to do, watch, eat or anything else with your partner fun and easy! at 2match, we simplify decision-making for couples, turning it into an enjoyable experience.

how it works?

swipe

both partners swipe through options like movies, restaurants, activities etc.

match

when you both swipe right on the same option, it’s a match!
